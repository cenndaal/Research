CPUS Formula

CPUS = wε·(1 − ε̂norm)  +  wmia·(1 − MIA_AUC)  +  wjsd·(1 − JSD)  +  wtstr·TSTR

All four terms are privacy-utility scores (higher = better). Inversions convert risk metrics into protection scores. Regulatory basis: GDPR Recital 26 & NZ Privacy Act 2020 IPP10.

6. REGULATORY FRAMING:
   Under GDPR Recital 26, data qualifies as "effectively anonymous"
   only when re-identification risk is negligible considering all
   reasonable means. MIA AUC > 0.6 and ε̂ > 1.0 suggest residual
   re-identification risk requiring documented DPIA / PIA controls.

   Under NZ Privacy Act 2020 IPP10, synthetic data shared externally
   must not be reasonably re-linkable. Models scoring REVIEW or NO GO
   require Privacy Officer sign-off before release.
